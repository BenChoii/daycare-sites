01 — Who we are

One of the most important stages of a child's life — handled with the warmth, care, and attention it deserves.

A Toronto tradition

Welcome to The Candy Factory.

Tucked into Bloordale Village at 1411 Bloor St West, The Candy Factory Day Care has been a sweet, familiar part of Toronto's west end for years. We're a licensed centre caring for children from infancy through their preschool years.

Our days are built around play, real food, fresh air and genuine relationships — the simple things that help a child feel safe enough to be curious. Families stay close to what's happening through daily photos and updates, so the connection never stops at our front door.

Open Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM – 6:00 PM, and proud to participate in the Canada-Wide Early Learning & Child Care system.

02 — Our programs

Room to grow at every age

Three age-based programs, each with its own gentle rhythm — from first steps to school-ready.

Program 01

Infants

0 – 17 months

Unhurried, loving care that follows your baby's own schedule — feeding, sleeping and plenty of cuddles, with photo updates so you never miss a milestone.

Program 02

Toddlers

18 – 29 months

A busy little world of movement, music and discovery, where growing minds explore textures, words and friendships in a safe, encouraging space.

Program 03

Preschool

30+ months

Hands-on learning that builds confidence and curiosity — early literacy, creativity and the social skills that get children excited and ready for kindergarten.
